Fairlady Aiko wasn't a one-off shoot. It was an experiment in running a social media account, built around a single Nissan Fairlady Z. The whole operation was the project: shooting the car, editing the clips, writing the captions, and keeping a posting rhythm to learn what an audience actually responds to.

The videography is what survives here. Low light, long lenses, and a lot of waiting around for the right pass.
